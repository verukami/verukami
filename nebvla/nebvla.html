Nebvla: HR dashboard design

There are a lot of new companies popping up trying to capture market share, Nebvla wanted to stand out with the launch of their platform.

Role

Digital product designer & Art director

Delirevables

Product strategy
UX strategy
UI Design

Year

2018

Review

The initial idea for this platform was solve RH challenges. We started to think from HR perspective of the process they needed to go through to hire a new employee and keep their history easily managed and in just one place.

I was asked to take on a creative role for this project. I was responsible to release all of the design efforts which included buildid a meaningful brand, designing the user interface & experience.



Challenges

The problem was clear. As a HR you got different challenges in day to day depend on the size of company that you are working so our main challenge was to estructure the content in a easy way.

Essentially they need a place where they can look the information of the employee with customise experience according of the need of the business.

Design process

User needs

Understanding the users’basic needs and problems was crucial in order to create a successful product that people would love to use.
We analyzed the market, interviiewed our target users and pinned down the major pain points on detailes user jpurney maps.

Branding

The brand needed to represent the name of the company and at the same time show that is a mexican product without falling in clichés. Midnight blue as primary color mixed with greens, magentas and cyans become the color palette. In addition we use a mix of geometrical structures with a nebulae inside for the logo.
Also was importat to find the perfect typography choices that would complement de color choices. I settled for Quicksand and Roboto after testing various typeface options. The contrast of these fonts aligned with the profesional approach of the dashboard.

Onboarding

With a few clicks simply upload the business info to a new personalized Nebvla.

HR solution

With constant communication & feedback of the user we’ve been developing Nebvla improving platform to make a perfect solution for companies.

UX Approach

We considered the entire user journey from discovery to actual real life usage. That include watching participants creating their process with their actual software and a design thinking session of three days where we deliver a set of rough wireframes that exposed how the dashboard should function from HR and employees. It was agreed that we want a dashboard that was different visually, less formal but still professional. We wanted to avoid the same graphics that are now in the market.

Menu

On of our most important goals was to keep the product as simple as possible. From beginning to end architecture planning process we focused on building an intuitive structure.

Schedule

When it comes the part of the schedules we did user inteviews with the HR people to identify what users were currently missing on the platform that they already use. We let 3 preloaded schedules and an extra option to bring the possibility of custom as much as needed for the user.
